List items that change background colors

Start: bottom of screen, end: after 100 pixels

The list items will go from blue rgb(0,0,255) to red rgb(255,0,0). As you can see in the source, they will start their transformation from being blue at "data-bottom-bottom" which is when the element's bottom is at the bottom of the screen. It will be completed (being red) when at "data--100-bottom-bottom" which is when the element's bottom is 100 pixels from the bottom of the screen (viewport).
